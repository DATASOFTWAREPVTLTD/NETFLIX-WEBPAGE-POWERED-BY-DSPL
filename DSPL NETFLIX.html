Frequently Asked Questions

How does Netflix work?

Netflix allows you to watch a wide variety of TV shows, movies, documentaries, and more on thousands of internet-connected devices. You can watch as much as you want, whenever you want, without a single commercial – all for one low monthly price. There's always something new to discover, and new TV shows and movies are added every week!

How much does Netflix cost?

Netflix offers three different membership plans that suit your needs. The basic plan starts at $9.99 per month, the standard plan at $13.99 per month, and the premium plan at $17.99 per month. Each plan allows you to enjoy unlimited TV shows and movies on your preferred devices.

Where can I watch?

You can watch Netflix on your TV, mobile device, or computer. All you need is an internet-connected device with the Netflix app or a browser that supports streaming. Netflix is also available on streaming devices such as smart TVs, game consoles, streaming media players, set-top boxes, and more.

How do I cancel?

Netflix is a subscription service with no commitment. You can easily cancel your Netflix membership online at any time, 24 hours a day. There are no cancellation fees, and you can reactivate your account anytime in the future without losing your account history or personalized recommendations.
